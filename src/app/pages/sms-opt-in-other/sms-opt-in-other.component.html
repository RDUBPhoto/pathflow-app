Pathflow SMS Opt-In Methods

Customers can opt in to receive SMS messages from Pathflow-supported businesses through approved consent methods.

Website Form Opt-In

Customers submit their phone number on the Pathflow web form and must check an explicit SMS consent checkbox before submitting.

Primary page: /sms-opt-in

Lead Widget Opt-In

Customers can opt in from an embedded lead widget on a business website by entering their phone number and enabling SMS consent.

Confirmation and Opt-Out

After opt-in, customers may receive a confirmation text. They can reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.
